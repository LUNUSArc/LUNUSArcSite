Biography

AJBLAkX is a designer, artist, and creative from Toledo, Ohio, in the American Midwest. He is also the founder and studio lead of LUNUS Arc.

His work can be described as a blend of Y2K cyber sigilism and neo-soul Afrocentrism on one hand, and abstract surrealism on the other.

AJBLAkX also vends at shows and other locations to showcase both his personal work and the work of LUNUS Arc. Follow him on social media to keep up with what is coming next.
